The Role of Websites in Local Businesses

Hannah Zhang

Overview Introduction Interviews Findings Analysis Implications References

Inquiry Approach

I used the case study research method in order to study specific Bay Area businesses and the situations they were dealing with in regards to their business growth and websites.


Data Collection

Multiple interviews were conducted where open ended questions were used to gain qualitative data to better understand the roles websites play in day to day business and in various stages of the marketing funnel. In total, six interviews were conducted as shown below.

Data Analysis

Coding was used to transform the information into meaningful and cohesive categories. This was done by analyzing the various ways businesses satisfied each stage of the marketing funnel, and the areas they were falling short.
