The Role of Websites in Local Businesses

Introduction Interviews Findings Analysis Implications References

Research Question

What are the various roles a website can play in a local business?


The Goal of This Paper

Websites are essential in communicating business missions, and their importance continues to increase with the onset of COVID-19. However, there are many factors in making a user friendly site, which deters businesses from utilizing a website to the maximum. Also, various businesses need help in different areas of the marketing funnel: awareness, interest, desire, action, and loyalty.

This research paper is going to tackle this problem by exploring the various roles websites are playing in current businesses, and then determine on a case-by-case basis, what specific businesses can change to accommodate their goals for growth. Ultimately, the mission is to increase understanding of the necessary factors to achieve various business goals, and apply this knowledge to help small businesses better thrive in the time of COVID-19.


Literary Review

The literary review seeks to introduce current research surrounding website expectations and look into current website options.
