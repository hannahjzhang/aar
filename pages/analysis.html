The Marketing Funnel


Website Roles in the Marketing Funnel

Based on interview results, I found parts of their websites that were helping in each stage of the marketing funnel. In the end, I found that small business websites were generally limited to identification information so they helped with awareness and interest. In medium sized business, their websites also had e-commerce options which stimulated desire and action. In larger businesses, there were many features customized to individuals which aided in the loyalty stage.
